    • About Me

    My name is Maya and I grew up in sunny San Diego, California. As a mixed child of a French mother and Sierra Leonean father, I was exposed to thier favorite foods which soon became mine. Growing up in many of the cities all over San Diego and in the mexican community, the Mexican culture and food has always made me feel at home.

    I left San Diego to move to Nantes, France in 2014. I was able to nurture great relationships with family that had been long overdue but when it came to good home cooked meals, I had to be inivative. Now, I live in Paris and have better access to imported ingredients that truly give a meal its authenticity. Over the years, I have had certain recipes that have pleased many locked away. However, I am in a sharing mood and I know for a fact eeach meal will have your belly full and satisfied.
