    • Guacamole

    Guacamole is great as a snack with chips or as a side for anything in a tortilla, to be honest. Tortillas are best when heated Sometimes when I can't find exactly tortillas, but whatever that equates to it like maybe wraps will do. This is a zesty and fresh side. It doeesn't take that long to do, aproximately 15 minutes. All you need is a chopping board, a knife, and a fork. It you have a food processor or blender, it will help with having a smooth and creamy texture. Personally, I like mine kind of chunky. I am not very big on percise measuremnts for ingredients, I add ingredient amounts based mainly on taste so I have mentioned ingredient amounts in genreral terms. Do what tastes right to you! After playing with this recipe, you gain better confidence with usind avocado in different meals making avodado toast or making avocado fritters and cakes.

    What you'll need:

    • avocados
    • limes or lime juice
    • taco seasoning or cumin
    • salt
    • tomatoes
    • red or purple onions
    • garlic
    • cilantro(optional)
    • sour cream(optional)
    • hot sauce or jalapenos(optional)

    For a serving of 4, you'd need approximately:

    • 3 avocados
    • 1 lime
    • 1 teaspoon of taco seasoning or cumin
    • 1 teaspoon of salt
    • 2 medium sized tomatoes
    • 1/2 a cup of diced onion
    • 1 teaspoon of garlic
    • 3 tablespoons of cilantro
    • 1 tablespoon of sour cream
    • 1/2 tablespoon of jalapenos or a few spalshes of hot sauce

    What to do with your ingredients:

    1. Dice your tomatoes, onions, and jalapenos if you ar using them
    2. Peel, pit, and mash your avocados with lime juice, salt,and the taco seasoning or cumin
    3. Add and mix in the tomatoes,onion,garlic,cilantro
    4. Stir in the sour cream and jalapenos
    5. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate between 30 minute - 1 hour for better flavor

    After last semester ended and we were on summer break, I finally had some free time to cook up a feast. I made plenty of servings for friends to enjoy and take home left overs. As you can see, I served it with a big bowl of chips, because there's never enough chips. You can eat it with chips and other salsas or scoop some guacamole on a quesidilla,taco,burrito... and enjoy!
